Leading Line

This photo was taken of a bridge near Cooley Landing. The lines formed by the shadows and the bars lead the viewer's eye towards the horizon.

Texture

This photograph is of the backside of a graphing calculator case. The texture is highlighted by lighting from the side, which reflects off of small bumps on the plastic.

Shape

The outline formed by the fence makes both geometric and organic shapes. The geometric shapes are formed by the straight sections and the organic by the curves.

Form

This scuplture has an interesting three-dimensional structure. The color scheme of this photograph is complimentary.

Color

This photograph was taken of maple leaves in mid autumn. The green color has alreay vanished from the edges of the leaves, leaving the tips a bright red color, but still remains, somewhat, in the center.

Value photo

This photograph was taken of three seashells and a piece of obsidian. To attain a monochromatic color scheme, I selected shells with a yellowish color and placed them on a black mat. Then, I lit it from the side using a white fluorescent lamp to highlight the shadows.

Space

This photograph has a large negative space at the top, which is filled with a smooth orange gradient caused by the sunset. I went onto the roof of my house to take this picture.
