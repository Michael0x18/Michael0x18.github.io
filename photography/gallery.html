Equality in diversity

My photo is called “Segregation in the Supermarket”. I went to the supermarket and saw this, so I took a picture of it to use for this class. What stood out to me is that while the products are certainly diverse, they are are not equal. The “Quaker” brand products are segregated from the ones whose mascot is a person of color. The portrayals are also different, with the Aunt Jemima brand pancake mix being based upon a racist stereotype. My photograph, though inconsequential in itself, mirrors the struggle for racial justice and equality that still continues to this day.
